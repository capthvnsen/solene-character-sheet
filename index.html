Backstory & Notes

Solène Kvällsrén grew up performing, honing her innate elven gift for reading rooms and her human scrappiness. She excels at making events and nights go perfectly, naturally wielding glamour magic and rogue skills. Her motivation is to protect loved ones while pursuing freedom and adventure. She is uniquely suited for infiltrating the evil wizard's mansion, treating it as a complex event to manage, ready with her Bag of Holding and a face that could belong to anyone.
